Lava Brown Sugar Cake

🔥包好後按扁，熱鍋刷油，煎到兩面金黃就可以吃啦🔥

食材：
麵糰：小麥粉290g，開水170g
餡：紅糖90g，小麥粉40g
Ingredients: Dough: 290g wheat flour, 170g boiling water Filling: 90g brown sugar, 40g wheat flour

🔥After wrapping, press flat, heat the pan with oil, fry until golden on both sides, you can eat it
